About Us

Pegahs Elephant Sanctuary is a sanctuary dedicated to the care and protection of elephants in need. Located in the heart of the tropical jungle, the sanctuary provides a safe haven for elephants who have been rescued from abusive or neglectful situations, or who are in need of a permanent home.

The sanctuary is home to a variety of elephants, including rescued street elephants, baby elephants who have been orphaned, and elderly elephants who can no longer work in the logging or tourism industries. Each elephant at the sanctuary receives individual care and attention from the dedicated team of animal caretakers, who are trained in the latest elephant care practices and are passionate about the well-being of these magnificent creatures.

Visitors to the sanctuary have the opportunity to interact with the elephants and learn about their natural behavior, habitats, and social structures. Guided tours are available that allow guests to observe the elephants in their natural habitat, and feed and bathe with them.

In addition to providing a safe haven for elephants, the sanctuary also works to educate the local community about the importance of elephant conservation and the dangers of illegal wildlife trafficking. The sanctuary runs various outreach programs and workshops to raise awareness about the plight of elephants and the importance of protecting these majestic creatures.

Pegahs Elephant Sanctuary is more than just a place for elephants to live, it is a place where these magnificent creatures can thrive in a safe and nurturing environment. The sanctuary is dedicated to providing the best possible care for the elephants in its care, and to promoting elephant conservation efforts in the local community and beyond.
