Tour Details

A tour at Pegahs Elephant Sanctuary is a unique and unforgettable experience. The tour begins with a guided walk through the lush jungle that surrounds the sanctuary, providing visitors with an opportunity to see the elephants in their natural habitat and learn about their behavior and social structures.

Next, guests are taken to the elephant feeding area where they can hand-feed the elephants, and participate in an educational talk with the sanctuary's knowledgeable animal caretakers. Visitors will learn about the individual stories of each elephant and how the sanctuary works to provide them with the best possible care.

One of the highlights of the tour is the elephant bath time, where guests can bathe with the elephants and watch as they play and splash in the water. This is a truly magical experience and a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to get up close and personal with these magnificent creatures.

Throughout the tour, guests are encouraged to ask questions and learn as much as they can about the elephants and the important work of the sanctuary. The tour concludes with a visit to the sanctuary's museum, where visitors can see displays about the history of elephants, their habitats, and the sanctuary's efforts to protect and conserve these magnificent creatures.

Overall, a tour at Pegahs Elephant Sanctuary is a wonderful opportunity to connect with nature, learn about elephant conservation, and have a truly unforgettable experience.
